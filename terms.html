1. What Reymont does

Reymont lets you read EPUB books in your browser. When you select a word, phrase or paragraph, the app can translate it into your chosen language and show context from the web: an AI-written summary with sources, a Wikipedia card and images from Wikimedia Commons. Selections are saved as highlights together with the answers, so you can reopen them later without repeating the request.
